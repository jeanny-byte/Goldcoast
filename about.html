Our Story

Empowering children through literacy since 2020

Our Mission

To enhance and increase child literacy across Ghana by providing accessible, engaging, and effective reading programs that empower children to become confident readers and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

A Ghana where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to develop strong literacy skills, enabling them to reach their full potential and contribute positively to society.

Our Journey

2020

Founded with a mission to improve child literacy in Ghana

2021

Launched our first reading program in Accra

2022

Expanded to 10 schools and 5 community centers

2023

Reached over 10,000 children across Ghana

Our Values

Excellence

Committed to providing the highest quality education and support to every child we serve.

Inclusivity

Ensuring our programs are accessible to all children regardless of their background or circumstances.

Compassion

Approaching our work with empathy and understanding for each child's unique journey.
